Cheval Blanc – Isle De France


St Barts


Previous Next

If we had to describe the Cheval Blanc hotel Isle De France in just one word, it would be ‘detail’. This hotel (or rather, palace) is made for those who appreciate the finer things. From the immaculate white sands of Flamands beach to its exquisite bathrobes, every inch of Isle de France oozes exclusivity and luxury.




Islands Magazine nights

As the sun begins to set, you’ll have the choice of two exceptional restaurants. La Cabane was new for 2019 and it’s perfect for a casual evening meal after a day at the beach – we highly recommend the burger. For an evening with a little more flair, La Case features the delectable creations of Jean Imbert. His menu explores French-West Indies cuisine, including extraordinary fruits from neighbouring islands.

Islands Magazine days

With one of the best beaches on St Barts at your feet, we can think of nothing better than relaxing on a sunbed all day. The hotel also has a fabulous spa and spectacular tropical gardens to explore.

Islands Magazine loves

Fancy waking up to the sound of the ocean? The Beach Suite leads straight out onto the sand. You’d expect the views from this sprawling suite to be incredible (and they are), but there is a surprise waiting for you on the terrace – the Beach Suite has its own infinity pool.
