St Barts


Beaches, beds and island beats

Follow our guide to find the perfect spot in paradise – and don’t forget to book ahead during peak season.

Find your holiday mode

After partying the night away on the beach, there’s nothing better than detoxing the next day at one of St Barts incredible spas.

Get the party started

St Barts has a wealth of enticing cocktail bars, live music venues and clubs to enjoy.

Hiking

Whether you’re hopping between bays, hiking to a secluded cove or taking a dip in the pools of water left at low tide, exploring St Barts on foot is endlessly rewarding.

Picnic at the beach

Believe it or not, you can still find little coves where there aren’t any bars or restaurants. Our recommendation? Pick one, pack a picnic and prepare to fall in love.

Set sail with Jicky Marine

You simply can’t visit the Caribbean without getting out on the water, and hiring a catamaran is the ultimate indulgence.

Surf’s up in St Barts

St Barts has so many fantastic beaches for catching waves, but let’s face it, a surfboard isn’t the most travel-friendly of luggage items.

Two words: Duty Free

Shopping is serious business in St Barts – and this has a lot to do with its Duty Free status.
