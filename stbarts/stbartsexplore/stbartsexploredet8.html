Two words: Duty Free


St Barts


Shopping is serious business in St Barts – and this has a lot to do with its Duty Free status. With huge designer labels – Hermes, Bulgari, Louis Vuitton – it’s like heading to New Bond Street, but without the tax, crowds or traffic. There’s even a Dolce and Gabbana store with a Martini bar inside. That being said, we tend to find ourselves in the smaller boutiques, where we’re guaranteed to find something truly unique.

Islands Magazine Loves

  • Baya .

    • Exquisite furnishings inspired by the owner’s travels around the world. You’ll find luxurious sunbeds, woven hammocks and hand-crafted lamps.

  • Yume .

    • One of a kind, statement clothing, jewellery and homewares with an ‘I just floated in from the beach’ vibe.

  • Sunbarth .

    • Before we hit the beach, we hit Sunbarth – our go-to store for men’s swimwear.

  • M’Bolo rum .

    • If you return from St Barts without a bottle of M’Bolo, did you even go? They’ve been infusing unique rums for over 25 years. We love their passion fruit and mango creation.

  • American Gourmet .

    • Stock up your villa fridge with an incredible choice of gourmet produce from around the world. You’ll find everything from fine cheeses, to waygu beef and caviar.
