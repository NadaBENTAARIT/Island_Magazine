Bill and Coo Coast Suites


Mykonos


Previous Next

By combining the amenities of a five-star hotel with the exclusivity of just 15 spacious suites, the owners of Bill and Coo Coast Suites have managed to create the perfect beach-holiday escape. It’s a grown up space, with neutral tones, private pools, personal trainers on request and the option to charter the hotel’s private yacht.




Islands Magazine nights

There are a wealth of dining options to choose from. As the sun begins to set, you could sit down to a traditional Greek feast in the Taverna, embark on an award-winning gastronomic project (this is 3km from the hotel, but a transfer is included) or enjoy a relaxed, waterfront meal at Beef Bar (the clue’s in the name). If you’re searching for something truly exclusive, Bill and Coo Coast also offers a moonlit private dining experience on the beach.

Islands Magazine days

The cocktail service begins at 11am (we’re sure it’s 5 o’clock somewhere…) and the hotel’s bespoke drinks are served poolside. With the beach at your feet plus massages in the gazebo, it’s the perfect place to spend a day ducking in and out of the sunshine.

Islands Magazine loves

We adore the spacious Coast Suite , which boasts its own outdoor pool, direct access to the beach and far-reaching views of the ocean.
