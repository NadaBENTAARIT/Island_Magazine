Hop cocktail bars at dusk


Ibisa


Previous Next

Bar hopping is something of an island pastime, especially if you want to sample the delicious tapas available as the sun sets. Head to the cobbled streets of the Old Town (Dalt Vila) to begin your evening adventure.




Islands Magazine Loves

Starting in style at S’Escalinata . We love sinking into their plush bean bags and sipping on fresh fruit cocktails as the sun goes down. Then we head to Petit Vermut , the island’s first vermuteria (vermouth bar) for a Negroni and homemade tapas, followed by Boodiou or Paradise Lost for some of Ibiza’s finest cocktails. If the night is still young, we’ll finish with tapas, wine and a lively atmosphere at Can Terra or La Bodega – both stay open until around 2am.
