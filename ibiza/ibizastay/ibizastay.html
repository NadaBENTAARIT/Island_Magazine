Ibiza


Even the world-famous party island has to sleep sometime – so why not do it in style? Ibiza’s accommodation has truly evolved, it’s now sleeker, smarter and more varied than ever before. The following hotels have been carefully picked by us to ensure you experience the very best that the ‘White Isle’ has to offer.

Psst… rather kick back in a villa? Talk to Niche Travel expert Ben about our VIP services. He’ll hook you up with the perfect place to stay, whether it’s a romantic getaway for two or a five star retreat with your family and friends. Discover how we could help you plan your dream villa holiday.

The Giri Residence

Effortlessly cool, but not pretentious. Luxurious, but wonderfully authentic. Secluded, but full of heart.

Seven Pines Kempinski Resort

We’re thrilled to introduce you to one of the island’s most exciting recent openings.

Can Xuxu Boutique Hotel

You simply can’t stop Alexandre Narakas. What was, for many years, a ranch-style property, inspired by his insatiable love of travel – we’re talking Javanese-style Villas with zebra prints on the walls, canopies over the beds and alfresco bathrooms.

Ca Na Xica Hotel & Spa

Some hotels are known for their food, others for their location and some simply have an aesthetic that stops you in your tracks. Ca Na Xica manages to deliver on all three.
